About Me

Hi! 😊, I’m Stephane, a 17 year old aspiring Software Developer and Data Analyst with a passion for solving problems through code and data. My interest in technology began in secondary school when I switched from IT to Computer Science, where I discovered how coding and systems could be used to build solutions. At first, I only achieved a grade 6 in my mock exams, but through persistence and focus I pushed myself to an 8 in my final results, an experience that showed me the value of determination and growth. Inspired by creators like Safiya Rozi and tech entrepreneurs, I began exploring projects of my own, starting with a Python game that taught me logical thinking, debugging, and user focused design. Since then, I’ve gained hands on experience with Python, SQL, and web development, and I’ve also worked as an IT Helpdesk Technician, collaborating with different teams to support system upgrades and troubleshoot technical issues. These experiences taught me not only technical skills, but also the importance of communication and teamwork in real world projects. Now, as a T Level Digital Production Design & Development student, my studies have further shaped my knowledge of programming, data analysis, and full stack development, giving me a strong foundation to build on. I enjoy creating practical projects that combine programming, data, and user friendly design, and I’m always learning new tools; from data visualization to web frameworks. I’m eager to apply my skills to real world challenges, contribute to collaborative teams, and continue growing as a developer and analyst.
